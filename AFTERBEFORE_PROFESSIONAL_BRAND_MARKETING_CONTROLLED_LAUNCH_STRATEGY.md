# AFTERBEFORE — PROFESSIONAL BRAND, MARKETING & CONTROLLED LAUNCH STRATEGY

**Status:** Launch Strategy  
**Scope:** Brand design, social media, influencer collaborations, guerrilla marketing, tourist acquisition and app-exclusive events  
**Primary Principle:** Brand attracts, content creates desire, exclusive experiences force first use, and product activity generates organic sharing.

---

## 1. Core Thesis

AfterBefore should not launch as another nightlife app.

It should launch as a cultural system that users can enter only through real experiences.

The launch engine is:

```text
Brand attracts
→ content creates desire
→ exclusive events create urgency
→ the app becomes the key to access
→ users complete quests and build reputation
→ achievements become shareable content
→ the community distributes AfterBefore organically
```

The strongest positioning is:

> **AfterBefore will be launched through real experiences, not promises.**

---

## 2. Why Professional Design Is Essential

For AfterBefore, professional design is not decoration.

## 19. App-Exclusive Access

The strongest early model is not necessarily native ticketing.

The recommended MVP structure is:

- event discovery may happen publicly,
- RSVP happens through AfterBefore,
- access status exists inside the app,
- QR pass exists inside the app,
- exact address may unlock inside the app,
- event quests exist inside the app,
- payment may use an external ticketing partner.

This keeps the app central without immediately creating full payment and fiscal complexity.

---

## 20. Native Ticketing Risk

Full native ticketing introduces:

- payment processing,
- refunds,
- fiscalization,
- QR ticket validation,
- merchant-of-record questions,
- settlement,
- customer support,
- fraud,
- legal responsibility.

It should not be added casually to the first launch.

---

## 21. Recommended MVP Ticketing Model

```text
Public teaser
→ AfterBefore RSVP
→ external payment if required
→ status confirmed inside app
→ digital pass
→ address unlock
→ rotating QR at entrance
```

This preserves product control while limiting early operational risk.

---

## 22. Secret Address Logic

Addresses should not be hidden only for hype.

Valid reasons include:

- privacy,
- capacity control,
- protection of temporary spaces,
- curated attendance,
- staged discovery,
- prevention of uncontrolled crowds.

Before joining, users should still know:

- approximate district,
- date,
- time,
- event type,
- price,
- entry rules,
- accessibility,
- cancellation rules,
- transportation options,
- care information.

### Example Unlock Flow

```text
T-48h
Access confirmed

T-12h
Approximate district unlocked

T-2h
Exact address unlocked

Entrance
Rotating QR pass
```

Public exhibitions should not always use secret addresses.

Mystery should remain limited and meaningful.
