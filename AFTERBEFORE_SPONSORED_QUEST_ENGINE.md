# AFTERBEFORE — SPONSORED QUEST ENGINE

**Status:** Revenue & Engagement Model  
**Scope:** Sponsored quests, community content, brand activations, rewards and creator progression  
**Primary Principle:** Brands do not buy passive attention. They fund participation, creativity and real community value.

---

## 1. Core Thesis

Sponsored Quest Engine allows brands to create structured challenges inside AfterBefore.

Users participate by completing a creative or social task, publishing content, tagging collaborators and contributing to the scene.

The sponsor provides rewards, while AfterBefore provides:

- quest design,
- campaign distribution,
- participation mechanics,
- moderation,
- community voting,
- anti-abuse controls,
- analytics,
- reward delivery support.

> **Brands no longer buy attention. They fund quests through which the community creates, participates and receives real value.**

---

## 2. Example Campaign

### Campaign Name

**Podržimo domaće — Najkul duo**

### Sponsor

A local fashion brand.

### Reward

Two friends win two outfits or selected clothing items from the sponsor.

### User Task

Participants submit a photo together, tag both people, explain where they would wear the outfits and select a relevant AfterBefore vibe tag.

### Example Prompt

> Objavi fotografiju sa drugaricom koja najbolje predstavlja vaš zajednički stil. Označite obe osobe, napišite gde biste nosile ove kombinacije i izaberite jedan AfterBefore vibe tag.

---

## 3. Campaign Flow

```text
Sponsor funds the quest and rewards
→ AfterBefore publishes a clearly labeled Sponsored Quest
→ two participants join together
→ they submit a photo and short story
→ the community reacts and votes
→ AfterBefore validates entries
→ finalists are selected
→ the winner receives the reward
→ the sponsor receives UGC, reach, engagement and campaign analytics
→ users receive XP, reputation and real rewards
```

## 5. Participation Requirements

For duo or group quests:

- every tagged person must approve participation,
- every participant must accept the submission,
- no one may be tagged without consent,
- all participants must meet age requirements,
- entries must follow campaign and community rules,
- duplicate and copied entries are rejected.

The submission is not public until all tagged participants approve it.

---

## 6. Winner Selection

The winner should not be selected only by likes.

A pure like-based model rewards existing follower counts and creates incentives for fake accounts, vote buying and manipulation.

### Recommended Hybrid Model

- **50% community votes**
- **25% originality and quality**
- **15% alignment with the campaign theme**
- **10% validity and contributor reputation**

### Simpler Version

1. The community selects the top five finalists.
2. The sponsor and AfterBefore select the winner from the finalists.

### Possible Award Categories

- Community Favorite
- Brand Choice
- AfterBefore Scene Choice
- Best Story
- Best Duo Energy
- Most Original Entry

This allows several participants to receive recognition without weakening the main prize.

## 15. Anti-Abuse Requirements

Every Sponsored Quest must include:

- clear **Sponsored** label,
- transparent rules,
- exact deadlines,
- public winner selection logic,
- participant consent,
- content moderation,
- fake-account detection,
- duplicate vote detection,
- rate limits,
- report and appeal mechanisms,
- prohibition of vote buying,
- prohibition of hidden paid promotion.

### Voting Safeguards

- one verified account per vote,
- optional check-in or account age requirement,
- suspicious voting pattern detection,
- no unlimited guest voting,
- no public exposure of detailed anti-fraud rules,
- audit log for finalist selection.

---

## 16. Content Rights

Participation should not automatically grant the sponsor permanent ownership of user content.

Recommended structure:

### Standard Participation

The content remains owned by the creator.

AfterBefore may display it inside the campaign according to platform terms.

### Sponsor Repost

The creator gives a separate opt-in for the sponsor to repost selected content.

### Paid Commercial Use

If the sponsor wants to use content in paid advertising, a separate agreement and compensation may be required.

### Core Rule

> **Entering a quest must not silently transfer permanent commercial rights to the sponsor.**

---

## 17. Community Integrity

Sponsored content must not damage the credibility of AfterBefore.

Sponsors may pay for:

- campaign infrastructure,
- clearly labeled distribution,
- rewards,
- creator activation,
- campaign analytics.

Sponsors may not pay for:

- fake community votes,
- artificial reputation,
- hidden advertising,
- removal of legitimate criticism,
- manipulation of organic venue or creator rankings.

> **Money buys campaign tools and distribution. It does not buy community trust.**
