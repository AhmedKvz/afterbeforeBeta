HOW IT WORKS · ONE NIGHT THROUGH THE APP

Before. Party. After.
The whole cycle — that's why the name.

01 · BEFORE — YOU PLAN
"Going out tonight?"
Home doesn't dump 50 cards on you — it suggests your night by taste and crew.
Set times — finally.
When the headliner actually plays. Entered by the crowd; the first contributor earns AFC.
Find your crew.
Nobody to go with? The app groups you with people heading to the same place — a group, not random 1:1.
Before quests.
A "before ritual" at a partner bar — cocktail on the house before the club.
02 · PARTY — THE FLOOR IS SACRED
Check in at the door.
GPS-verified arrival = proof of presence. Earns XP and AFC, unlocks everything else.
Spark → Match.
See who's here (with their consent), send a spark — mutual opens a chat. The window lasts as long as the night does.
Dance Floor score.
Your phone measures how hard you go — from your pocket. Nightly leaderboard. Dancing gets measured, not filmed.
Phone stays down.
No filming on the floor. Everything the app does during the party, it does from your pocket.
03 · AFTER — CAMERAS OUT, VALUE COMES BACK
The after is camera territory.
Content campaigns: best after photo, best before video — the scene votes, the sponsor pays the prize.
First-hand reviews.
Your take becomes the guide for the next crowd — and for tourists who'd otherwise be reading Reddit.
AFC lands in your balance.
Everything you contributed that night — check-in, set times, dancing, a review — turns into points.
Real-world rewards.
Drinks, skip-the-line, tickets, trips — claimed in person, at partner venues.
THE HEART OF THE SYSTEM

The circular economy of the night.

This is what we're starting: a closed loop where everyone who gives — gets. Clubbers give presence and content. Venues give rewards and space. Brands fund the pool. And value circulates — instead of leaking out.

CLUBBERS GIVE → GET
Check-ins, set times, reviews, dancing, after content → AFC points → drinks, entry, tickets, trips. Going out starts paying you back.
VENUES GIVE → GET
Guest lists, drinks, discounts → a verified crowd at the door + live data (when it peaks, what works, who returns).
BRANDS GIVE → GET
The prize pool (tickets, trips, merch) → authentic content and measurable presence — not clicks, but real people in real places.
⚖️ THE IRON RULE
No reward gets "printed". Every payout is backed by real money — from a venue, a sponsor, or a tourist. That's why this isn't another app with worthless points: AFC has backing, like air miles — but for nightlife.
GIVING BACK TO THE COMMUNITY

The scene isn't an audience. The scene is the owner.

Platforms treat nightlife as content to scroll. We treat it as a community that deserves a voice, safety, and a share of the value.

🗳
The crowd decides
Party of the month, the app's direction, campaign winners — the community votes, not the organizer. Verified votes only (at least 1 check-in) — no farms.
🛡
Safety built in
Ghost mode (the count shows, your identity is your choice), block & report everywhere, group meetups instead of random 1:1. Care, not lecturing.
📵
The floor is sacred
Platform rule: no filming on the dancefloor. Cameras come out at the after. A tool that protects the reason we go out.
🌱
Young people earn from their culture
Up-and-coming DJs, creators, the people who carry the scene — get a stage, an audience, and income. A culture that feeds the people who make it.
THE VISION · LAYER BY LAYER

From one night in Belgrade — to the economy of nightlife.

LAYER 1
NOW · LIVE
Proof of the loop — Belgrade beta
Discovery, check-in, spark/match, quests, set times, dance floor, crews, first campaigns. Everything you see in the beta — already works.
LAYER 2
Afters — opening up after midnight
Peak demand, zero supply: where to after? A host opens their after to vetted people (invited by reputation, no public address) — entry/contribution goes to the host. Afters become safe and worth hosting instead of chaotic. Monetizing the after = the first marketplace move.
LAYER 3
Marketplace — the night as a livelihood
Be My Guide — locals guide tourists through the real scene (tourists are the paying demand; locals earn from what they know).
Talent — DJs, photographers, video creators: booked directly through the app, with reputation instead of a CV.
Helpers — the people who carry the night: setup, cloakroom, promo, safe rides. Every night employs people — now formally too.
LAYER 4
Cities — same OS, new scene
Budapest, Istanbul, Berlin, Barcelona. Every city gets its own scene-community on the same system — locals first, always.
